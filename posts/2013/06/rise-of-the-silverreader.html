Rise of the SilverReader

Published on Friday, June 28, 2013
projects silverreader

Yes, you read that right. Rise of the SilverReader, not Rise of the Silver Surfer.

Right after the news that shocked the world (at least our world) my friend Radenko Zec suggested that we build an alternative to Google Reader. So, few months ago, together with another friend of ours Igor Markovic, we started working on it and apparently, we weren’t alone.

And now, just few days before our beloved Google Reader shuts down, we have published our first live beta version!

You can read the official announcement on SilverReader blog. Also, Radenko has made a blog post explaining a technical side of the story.

Try the SilverReader now. If you need any help, or want to submit an idea or issue, visit SilverReader Knowledge Base.

Enjoy!
